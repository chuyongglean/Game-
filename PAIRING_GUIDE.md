# How to Initiate Pairing

Follow these steps to connect your mobile controller to the GameStation:

1. **Launch the Game**: Open `index.html` on your desktop browser. 
2. **Get the Code**: Look at the "Ready to Sync?" overlay. It will display a unique 6-character **Room Code** (e.g., `A7B2X1`).
3. **Open Controller**: Open the separate Controller Web App on your smartphone.
4. **Enter Code**: In the Controller App, enter the Room Code shown on your desktop.
5. **Sync**: Press "Connect" on the controller. 
6. **Confirmation**: Once connected, the desktop overlay will disappear, and the status bar will turn green, indicating "Controller Paired".
7. **Play**: Move the joystick or press buttons on your phone to control the square in the game arena.
